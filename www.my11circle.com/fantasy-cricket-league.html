Cricket Match Schedule and Details

The England cricket team is scheduled to tour South Africa from December 2019 to Feb 2020. Both the countries are set to play four test matches and three odi matches and three twenty20 international matches. Team will also play 2-day and 3-day practice matches before 26th Dec.

» West Indies Tour of India 2019

West Indies cricket team will be playing three One Day International and three T20 International matches in December 2019. Prior to this West India played against Afghanistan in November 2019.

» Big Bash League 2019-20

Big Bash League (BBL) 2019-20 is scheduled between December 2019 to February 2020. It is the ninth edition of the domestic T20 Cricket tournament held in Australia. This season will have more matches i.e 61 compared to previous season 59.

» Ranji Trophy 2019-20

Ranji Trophy 2019-20 will be the 86th Edition of the first-class tournament. The schedule is between December 2019 to March 2020 taking place in India. For the first time, Chandigarh will be playing.

» MOMENTUM ONE DAY CUP 2020

Six team from across the country compete in Momentum One Day Cup In South Africa. Matches are usually played partially under the lights as day-night matches. Momentum One Day Cup 2020 will be 39th edition to be held in February.

» Bangladesh tour of India 2019

Bangladesh cricket team is scheduled to play two Test matches and three T20 International matches on November 2019. The test series are part of 2019-21 ICC World Test Championship. This is the first time Bangladesh will play T20 International series in India against India.

» WOMEN'S BIG BASH LEAGUE 2019

The 2019–20 Women's Big Bash League season or WBBL 05 will be the fifth season of Women's Big Bash League, the semi-professional women's Twenty20 domestic cricket competition in Australia.

» PLUNKET SHIELD 2019-20

The 2019–20 will be the 91st season of the Plunket Shield, the domestic first-class cricket competition in New Zealand. It is scheduled to take place between October 2019 and March 2020.

» South Africa Tour of India 2019

The South Africa cricket team is scheduled to tour India in September and October 2019 to play three Tests and three Twenty20 International (T20I) matches.

» Bangladesh Tri-Nation Series 2019

The Bangladesh Tri-Nation Series 2019 is a cricket tournament that will take place in September 2019. All the matches being played as Twenty20 Internationals.

» T20 Blast 2019

The Vitality Blast 2019 is a professional Twenty20 cricket league currently being played in England and Wales.

The Ashes 2019 is Test cricket matched played between England and Australia. The venues are Edgbaston, Lord's, Headingly, Old Trafford and The Oval.

» India Tour of West Indies 2019

The India cricket team touring the West Indies and the United States during August and September 2019 to play two Tests, three One Day Internationals and three Twenty20 International matches

Scotland Tri Series 2019 between Oman, Papua New Guinea and the Scotland cricket teams, with the matches played as One Day International fixtures. All of the matches formed part of the 2019–22 ICC Cricket World Cup League 2

The ICC Cricket World Cup 2019 was One Day International cricket tournament by men's national teams. It was hosted by England and Wales.
