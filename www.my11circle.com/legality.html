Legality

Is playing My11Circle legal?

Yes, it is absolutely safe! My11Circle is a game of “skill”. The relevant law in India is absolutely clear that, unlike games of "chance", games which substantially require a player to apply his skill are absolutely legal.

All games hosted by My11Circle are carefully designed and the jurisdiction of prize winners is strictly monitored to ensure legal compliance with relevant laws currently in force in India.

If you are accessing this site from Andhra Pradesh, Assam, Orissa, Telangana, Sikkim or Nagaland, we hope you will enjoy our practice contests. However, the law in these states is unclear on playing games of skill with entry fees for cash prizes. You are therefore prohibited from playing cash contests on My11Circle from any of these states.
