Key players to watch out for

Faf Du Plessis – The South African had a good first season as a skipper last year although his bat didn’t produce as many runs as it used to do for Chennai. With him trying to make a comeback into the South African side, Du Plessis will look to have a memorable tournament both as a leader and a batter.

Shahbaz Ahmed – The all-rounder from Bengal came into his own in the last year’s Indian T20 League. While he was expected to deliver as a bowler, his gritty knocks with the bat and his ability to soak in pressure showed that Bangalore have a great asset with them who they must groom in the coming years.

Virat Kohli – The star batsman might be in the last stage of his T20 career but he still remains the man who brings people to the ground when it comes to Bangalore. And his experience was evident in that epic encounter against Pakistan in the ICC T20 World Cup last year. With him coming into this tournament after having regained form, Bangalore fans will have a reason to hope this year.

Squad Analysis of Bangalore

Bangalore finished the last Indian T20 League third but had their hiccups throughout the tournament. On many occasions, it was Dinesh Karthik’s finishing that saved them. With the ball, Wanindu Hasaranga had a huge responsibility and he delivered on most occasions by taking 26 wickets. The team didn’t go for a lot of purchases on the auction table but added two Englishmen to their setup in the form of Will Jacks and Reece Topley. Unfortunately, Jacks got injured right before the league and will be unavailable for Bangalore.

Strengths – The batting lineup stlll looks impressive and Glenn Maxwell, who is recovering from injury, would need to replicate his last year’s form this year too. The lower order was crucial to Bangalore’s success last year and they found a star in Rajat Patidar. However, it would put too much pressure on the lower order if the top-order fails to fire repeatedly as it happened last year.

Weaknesses This will be the first time this bowling lineup will bowl at the Chinnaswamy Stadium, a daunting task for any bowling attack. Considering how well Yuzvendra Chahal did at the same ground year after year for Bangalore, Wanindu Hasaranga will know he has big shoes to fill this year in Bangalore colors. But the small boundaries of the ground could neutralize the threat of his variations.

At the top, both Virat Kohli and Faf Du Plessis are slightly similar as batters who like to take some time before accelerating. That can hurt Bangalore in case their lower middle order doesn’t deliver on the promise it packs. The inclusion of Jacks in the XI could have really helped the team, but an injury to him means that Bangalore will have to look for other options and hope that Maxwell has recovered fully.

Bangalore Team in Indian T20 League 2023

For the 16th time in the Indian T20 League, Bangalore will start with the hope of winning their first ever title. The team that finished third last year will hope that 2023 is their year finally. A major change took place last year for Bangalore when South Africa’s Faf Du Plessis came out for the toss as captain instead of Virat Kohli. Du Plessis led well but the title still eluded them despite the team finding many positives in their campaign.

But finding positives shouldn’t be the only goal of the franchise this year and giving the fans the title that they have been waiting for so long should be Bangalore’s number 1 priority this year. Traditionally, the team hasn’t been a pushover and has made it to the finals of the tournament multiple times since their first final appearance in 2009. However, that has only added to the frustration of the fans who now have accumulated enough heartbreaks in these years and would like the team to get over the line for once. The team has big stars in the form of Virat Kohli, Faf Du Plessis and Glenn Maxwell but it’s the bowling where the team has grown considerably by adding Wanindu Hasaranga and finding Harshal Patel in his peak. The addition of the tall left-arm quick from England, Reece Topley, will further strengthen the bowling attack.
As always, Virat Kohli’s form will remain the main talking point in the team’s campaign and the master batter would hope for a better campaign than last year where he only managed two half-centuries. The fans however will be mighty pleased to see him come back to form in the recent months where he has brought up five centuries in multiple formats.

Bangalore’s stats in Indian T20 League

    • Bangalore have finished as the finalists in the tournament in 2009, 2011 and 2016.
    • Virat Kohli is the only player who has played 15 seasons for only one team in the Indian T20 League.
    • Not only does Virat Kohli sit on the top of the all-time run charts in the Indian T20 League, he also holds the record of most runs scored by a player in a single season. He achieved this feat in 2016 when he scored 973 runs in the tournament.
    • Bangalore also holds the record for the highest ever team score in the tournament. They scored 263/5 in the 2013 edition of the tournament.
    • The team also holds the dubious record of the lowest score in the tournament’s history when they were all out for 49.
    • Virat Kohli along with AB De Villiers made a partnership of 229 playing against Gujarat in 2016, the highest in the league’s history. In 2015, they had made another 200+ partnership against Mumbai.
